Bathroom Cleaning

Our dedicated team will thoroughly clean your bathroom from top to bottom. This includes removing cobwebs from corners and washing or vacuuming floors. We'll also dust every part of the bathroom to prevent dirt and germs from building up on surfaces like towel racks, toilet paper holders, blinds, windowsills, doors, doorframes, cabinet fronts, and baseboards..

Our bathroom services include:

  • Scrubbing and rinsing sinks and showers
  • Removing stubborn soap scum buildup
  • Disinfecting toilets inside and out
  • Wiping down vanities and mirrors

Bedroom Cleaning

If finding time to clean your bedroom regularly is a challenge in your busy life, our team of home cleaning professionals is here to assist you. Our bedroom cleaning service will turn your space into a fresh and inviting room, perfect for relaxation and a peaceful night's sleep. Deep cleaning your bedroom can make all the difference in transforming it from a place you might dread into a serene sanctuary.

Our bedroom services include:

  • Wipe down corners to remove any cobwebs from corners.
  • Vacuum carpets and wash all hardwood, laminate, or tile floors.
  • Dry wood floors.
  • Vacuum furniture and under cushions.
  • Bed-making and changing bed linens.
  • Dust end tables, television stands, bookshelves, and other pieces of furniture, as well as any decorative accessories.

Kitchen Cleaning

Our services include:

  • Clean and wipe your countertops to remove crumbs.
  • Scrub the cooktops.
  • Thoroughly clean your microwave inside and out.
  • Remove fingerprints and smudges.
  • Make faucets and cabinet hardware shine.
  • Vacuum and mop the floors.
  • Ensure your oven is spotless inside and out.
